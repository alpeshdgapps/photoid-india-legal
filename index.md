# Privacy Policy for PhotoID India

**Last updated: Jun 26, 2026**

PhotoID India ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed and operated by Logictray Mobile Lab ("we" or "the developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PhotoID India mobile application.

By using PhotoID India, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

PhotoID India is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information, and most app data is stored locally on your device.

### 1.1 Photos and Images

When you use PhotoID India, you capture photos for processing into document-size ID photos. These photos:

- Are processed on your device for the free preview using on-device face detection and preview processing
- Are stored locally on your device unless you choose to delete them
- Are not stored on servers we operate
- Are securely sent to a third-party AI processing provider (see Section 2) only when you explicitly request a paid studio-quality export, and only so that provider can generate your requested result on our behalf
- Are used only to generate your requested ID photo output
- Can be deleted at any time through **Settings → Clear all photos**

For paid studio-quality export, the photo you capture is temporarily processed by a third-party AI processing provider to remove or clean up the background. The provider processes the photo you capture only to generate your requested result and return it to the app. We do not authorize the provider to use your photo for advertising, resale, or any independent purpose unrelated to providing the processing service. We do not sell your photos or share them with third parties for their own use. Details of the provider, including its data retention, are described in Section 2.

### 1.2 In-App Purchase Information

When you purchase credits through Google Play Billing, Google handles the payment transaction. We do not receive or store your payment method details, credit card numbers, UPI ID, or bank account information.

The app may receive and store limited purchase-related information needed to grant and manage credits, such as:

- A purchase token confirming your transaction
- The product identifier for the credit pack purchased
- Purchase timestamp
- Local credit balance and local purchase state

Your purchased credits are stored locally on your device. Credits do not expire while stored on this device. Because credits are stored locally, uninstalling the app, clearing app data, or changing devices may remove remaining credits unless restoration is available in the app.

### 1.3 Crash Reports and Analytics

PhotoID India integrates Firebase services operated by Google to improve app reliability and user experience.

**Firebase Crashlytics** may collect crash and diagnostic data when the app unexpectedly closes, such as:

- Stack traces and crash details
- Device model and Android version
- App version at the time of crash
- Diagnostic information needed to fix app errors

**Firebase Analytics** may collect app usage events, such as:

- Which screens are viewed
- Which document types are selected
- Whether the paywall is opened
- Which purchase pack is selected
- Whether purchases are completed or abandoned
- Session duration and frequency

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics do not receive photos, faces, names, phone numbers, contact details, or payment card details from PhotoID India. These services may collect app-instance identifiers, device information, approximate location metadata such as country, region, or city, crash diagnostics, and usage events to help us improve reliability and user flow.

### 1.4 App Configuration and Security Signals

PhotoID India may use Firebase Remote Config, Firebase App Check, Firebase Installations, and related Firebase/Google services to deliver app configuration, protect backend access, reduce abuse, and improve reliability.

These services may process technical identifiers, approximate location metadata, device/app information, or device/app integrity signals. They do not receive your photos.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 AI Processing Provider (Paid Studio-Quality Export)

When you use credits and request a studio-quality version of your photo, the photo you capture is sent through our processing flow to our third-party AI processing provider. Our current provider is Replicate (replicate.com), a cloud AI processing service operated by Replicate Inc. We may change this provider in the future; if we do, we will update this section.

The provider is used only to produce the requested background-removal or background-cleanup result. The provider processes the photo you capture on our behalf as a service provider and returns the processed result to the app. We do not authorize the provider to use your photo for advertising, resale, or any independent purpose unrelated to providing the processing service. We do not sell your photos or share them with third parties for their own use.

- Your photo is transmitted over HTTPS using encrypted connections
- The provider processes the photo and returns the result
- We do not store your photos on servers we operate
- We do not use your photos to train machine learning models
- For API-based processing with our current provider, the provider states that input parameters, output values, output files, and logs are automatically removed after about one hour by default

The provider processes data according to its own terms and privacy policy. For the current provider's privacy policy, see:
https://replicate.com/privacy

### 2.2 Firebase (Crashlytics, Analytics, Remote Config, App Check)

PhotoID India uses Firebase services operated by Google LLC:

- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Crash reporting and diagnostic service
- **Firebase Analytics**: Usage analytics to understand app flow and improve the product
- **Firebase Remote Config**: App configuration without requiring a full app update
- **Firebase App Check / Firebase Installations**: App integrity, abuse prevention, and SDK support

Firebase services are governed by Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase data processed for PhotoID India does not include photos, faces, names, phone numbers, contact details, or payment card details.

### 2.3 Google Play Services

Google Play Services handles in-app purchases, app updates, installation, and related Play services. Google Play handles payment processing, purchase records, and refunds according to Google's policies.

Google's privacy practices are described at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 5. Data Retention

**Photos on your device**: Retained until you delete them through **Settings → Clear all photos**, delete exported files manually, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

**Generated exports**: PDFs or JPEGs saved inside app-controlled storage may be deleted through the app. Files saved to public folders such as Downloads are controlled by you and may need to be deleted manually from your file manager.

**Purchase records and credits on your device**: Retained locally until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Credits do not expire while stored on this device. Because credits are stored locally, uninstalling the app, clearing app data, or changing devices may remove remaining credits unless restoration is available in the app.

**Photos sent for paid processing**: When you request a paid studio-quality export, the photo is sent to our third-party AI processing provider and is subject to that provider's data retention. See Section 2 for the current provider and its stated retention.

**Crash reports and diagnostics**: Retained by Google/Firebase according to Firebase's applicable retention policies.

**Analytics data**: Retained by Google/Firebase according to Firebase's applicable retention policies. Analytics data used by PhotoID India does not include photos, faces, names, phone numbers, contact details, or payment card details.

**Google Play purchase history**: Retained by Google according to Google Play policies and is outside our direct control.

## 6. Children's Privacy

PhotoID India is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app and you need help removing associated local data, please contact us and we will guide you through deleting it from the device.

The paid studio-quality export feature relies on a third-party AI processing provider, as described in Section 2. That provider's terms require users to be 18 or older, or to use the service only with the consent and under the responsibility of a parent or guardian.

If you are under 18, you should use PhotoID India only with the involvement of a parent or guardian. You should not use the paid studio-quality export feature unless your parent or guardian consents to that use and accepts responsibility under the applicable third-party terms.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

**Deleting your photos**: Use **Settings → Clear all photos** within the app. This permanently deletes photos and app-managed generated files stored by the app on your device.

**Deleting exported files**: If you saved PDFs or JPEGs to public folders such as Downloads, you may need to delete those files manually from your device's file manager.

**Uninstalling the app**: Removes locally stored app data, including photos, purchase records, credit balance, generated app-managed files, and settings.

**Requesting data deletion by email**: If you need assistance with data deletion or have questions, contact us at photoid.india.support@gmail.com. Because most user data is stored locally on your device and not on servers we operate, we will guide you through deletion steps. We cannot remotely delete data stored only on your device.

**Purchase refunds**: Refund requests are handled through Google Play according to Google's refund policies.

**Detailed deletion instructions**: See our full data deletion guide:
https://alpeshdgapps.github.io/photoid-india-legal/data-deletion

- Paid studio-quality processing is handled through a backend processing flow rather than exposing AI provider secrets in the app

However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
