# Privacy Policy for PhotoID India

**Last updated: May 30, 2026**

PhotoID India ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed and
operated by Logictray Mobile Lab ("we" or "the developer"). This
Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you
use the PhotoID India mobile application.

By using PhotoID India, you agree to the practices described in
this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

PhotoID India is designed with privacy as a core principle. We
collect minimal information, and most of your data never leaves
your device.

### 1.1 Photos and Images

When you use PhotoID India, you take photos for processing into
government ID format images. These photos:

- Are processed on your device using on-device face detection and
  segmentation technology for the free preview
- Are stored only in the app's local storage on your device
- Are never uploaded to our servers under any circumstances
- Are sent to Replicate (a third-party AI service) only when you
  explicitly purchase and request a studio-quality version
  (see Section 2.1)
- Can be deleted at any time through Settings → Clear all photos

### 1.2 In-App Purchase Information

When you purchase credits through Google Play Billing, Google
handles the payment transaction entirely. We receive only:

- A purchase token confirming your transaction
- The product identifier (which credit pack was purchased)
- Purchase timestamp

We do not receive or store your payment method details, credit
card numbers, UPI ID, or bank account information. All payment
processing is handled exclusively by Google Play.

Your purchased credits (Single Photo, Pack of 5, or 100 Photos
Pack) never expire and are stored locally on your device.

### 1.3 Crash Reports and Analytics

PhotoID India integrates Firebase (by Google) for two purposes:

**Firebase Crashlytics**: Automatically collects anonymous crash
reports when the app unexpectedly closes. This includes:
- The line of code where the crash occurred (stack trace)
- Device model and Android version
- App version at time of crash
- No personally identifiable information, no photos, no
  purchase data

**Firebase Analytics**: Collects anonymous usage data to help us
understand how users interact with the app. This includes:
- Which screens are viewed and in what order
- Which document types are selected
- Whether the paywall is opened and which pack is selected
- Whether purchases are completed or abandoned
- Session duration and frequency
- No photos, no faces, no personal information of any kind

Both services collect only anonymous, aggregated data. No photos
or personal data are included in crash reports or analytics
events. You cannot be individually identified from this data.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Replicate (for Paid Studio-Quality Output)

When you purchase credits and request a studio-quality version
of your photo, your selected photo is sent to Replicate
(replicate.com), a cloud AI processing service operated by
Replicate Inc.

- Replicate processes your photo to produce a high-quality
  background-removed version
- The processed photo is returned to your device
- Photos sent to Replicate are automatically deleted from
  Replicate's servers within 1 hour of the prediction completing,
  per their data retention policy
- Your photo is transmitted over HTTPS using encrypted connections
- See Replicate's privacy policy: https://replicate.com/privacy

### 2.2 Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics)

PhotoID India uses Firebase services operated by Google LLC:

- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Crash reporting service that collects
  anonymous diagnostic data when the app crashes
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage analytics to understand
  app performance and user flow
- **Firebase Remote Config**: Used to deliver app configuration
  (such as product display information) without requiring a full
  app update. No personal data is collected through Remote Config.

Firebase services are governed by Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase data is processed in accordance with Google's data
processing terms and does not include any photos, faces,
purchase details, or personally identifiable information.

### 2.3 Google Play Services

Google Play Services handles in-app purchases, app updates, and
installation. Google's privacy practices are described at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 3. Permissions We Request

PhotoID India requests these device permissions:

- **Camera**: Required to capture photos for ID processing.
  Without this permission, the app cannot function.
- **Storage**: Required to save processed photos and generated
  PDFs to your device.
- **Internet**: Required to send photos to Replicate for paid
  studio-quality processing, and to communicate with Firebase
  services for crash reporting and analytics.

We do not request access to:
- Your contacts
- Your location
- Your microphone
- Other apps' data
- Your call logs or SMS

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect solely to:

- Process your photos as you direct
- Verify and grant in-app purchases
- Diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues (via Crashlytics)
- Understand which features are used and improve the app
  (via Analytics)
- Deliver updated app configuration without a full app update
  (via Remote Config)

- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your photos to train machine learning models
- Share your photos with anyone other than Replicate (only when
  you explicitly request paid processing)
- Use crash data or analytics to identify individual users
- Use your information for advertising or marketing without
  explicit consent

## 5. Data Retention

**Photos on your device**: Retained until you delete them via
Settings → Clear all photos, or until you uninstall the app.

**Purchase records on your device**: Retained locally until you
uninstall the app. Credits purchased never expire — they remain
valid indefinitely.

**Photos sent to Replicate**: Automatically deleted from
Replicate's servers within 1 hour of processing completing.

**Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)**: Retained by Google per
Firebase's data retention policy (typically 90 days). Crash data
is anonymous and contains no photos or personal information.

**Analytics data (Firebase Analytics)**: Retained by Google per
Firebase's data retention policy. Analytics data is anonymous
and aggregated. No photos or personal information are included.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

**Deleting your photos**: Use Settings → Clear all photos within
the app. This permanently deletes all photos stored by the app
on your device. Since your photos are stored locally on your
device and not on our servers, this is the complete and
immediate deletion mechanism.

**Uninstalling the app**: Removes all locally stored data
including photos, purchase records, and generated PDFs.

**Requesting data deletion by email**: If you need assistance
with data deletion or have questions, contact us at
photoid.india.support@gmail.com. Because your data is stored
entirely on your device (not on our servers), we will guide you
through the in-app deletion steps. We cannot delete data
remotely since we have no access to data on your device.

**Purchase refunds**: Request through Google Play within their
refund policy window (typically 48 hours for in-app purchases).

**Detailed deletion instructions**: See our full data deletion
guide at:
https://alpeshdgapps.github.io/photoid-india-legal/data-deletion

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your
information:

- All network communications use HTTPS encryption in transit
- Photos and app data are stored in Android's app-private
  internal storage, isolated by the operating system from
  other applications
- We do not log or persist your photos on any servers we operate
- Crash reports and analytics data contain no photos or personal
  identifiers

However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee
absolute security.
