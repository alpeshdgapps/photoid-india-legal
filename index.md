# Privacy Policy for PhotoID India

**Last updated: May 25, 2026**

PhotoID India ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed and 
operated by Logictray Mobile Lab ("we" or "the developer"). This Privacy 
Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the 
PhotoID India mobile application.

By using PhotoID India, you agree to the practices described in 
this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

PhotoID India is designed with privacy as a core principle. We 
collect minimal information, and most of your data never leaves 
your device.

### 1.1 Photos and Images

When you use PhotoID India, you take or import photos for 
processing into government ID format images. These photos:

- Are processed on your device using on-device face detection and 
  segmentation technology
- Are stored only in the app's local storage on your device
- Are never uploaded to our servers, except when you explicitly 
  choose to generate a paid studio-quality version (see Section 2)
- Can be deleted at any time through the app's Settings → Clear 
  all photos option

### 1.2 In-App Purchase Information

When you purchase credits through Google Play Billing, Google 
handles the payment transaction. We receive only:
- A purchase token confirming your transaction
- The product identifier (which credit pack was purchased)
- Purchase timestamp

We do not receive or store your payment method details, credit 
card numbers, UPI ID, or bank account information. All payment 
processing is handled by Google Play.

### 1.3 Technical Information

We do not currently collect analytics, crash reports, or usage 
data. We may add anonymous crash reporting in future versions 
(via Firebase Crashlytics or similar), in which case this policy 
will be updated and you will be notified of the change.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Replicate (for Paid Studio-Quality Output)

When you purchase credits and request a studio-quality version 
of your photo, your selected photo is sent to Replicate 
(replicate.com), a cloud AI processing service operated by 
Replicate Inc.

- Replicate processes your photo to produce a high-quality 
  background-removed version
- The processed photo is returned to your device
- Replicate's processing servers do not retain your photo after 
  the processing is complete (per their published policy)
- Your photo is sent over HTTPS using encrypted connections
- See Replicate's privacy policy at https://replicate.com/privacy

### 2.2 Google Play Services

Google Play Services handles in-app purchases, app updates, and 
installation. Google's privacy practices are described at 
https://policies.google.com/privacy.

## 3. Permissions We Request

PhotoID India requests these device permissions:

- **Camera**: Required to capture photos for ID processing. 
  Without this permission, the app cannot function.
- **Storage**: Required to save processed photos and generated 
  PDFs to your device.
- **Internet**: Required to send photos to Replicate for paid 
  studio-quality processing.

We do not request access to:
- Your contacts
- Your location
- Your microphone
- Other apps' data
- Your call logs or SMS

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect solely to:
- Process your photos as you direct
- Verify and grant in-app purchases
- Provide technical support if you contact us

We do not:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your photos to train machine learning models
- Share your photos with anyone other than Replicate (only when 
  you explicitly request paid processing)
- Use your information for advertising or marketing without 
  explicit consent

## 5. Data Retention

- **Photos on your device**: Retained until you delete them via 
  Settings → Clear all photos, or until you uninstall the app
- **Purchase records on your device**: Retained for the lifetime 
  of your credits (e.g., 30 days for unlimited pack)
- **Photos sent to Replicate**: Not retained by Replicate after 
  processing completes (typically within seconds)

## 6. Children's Privacy

PhotoID India is not directed to children under the age of 13. 
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. 
If you are under 18, please use this app under parental 
supervision.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You can:
- Delete all photos and data through Settings → Clear all photos
- Uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data
- Request a refund through Google Play within their refund policy 
  window (typically 48 hours)
- See our detailed data deletion instructions at: 
  https://alpeshdgapps.github.io/photoid-india-legal/data-deletion
- Contact us at photoid.india.support@gmail.com for any privacy-related concerns

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your 
information:
- All network communications use HTTPS encryption
- Locally stored credentials use Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
- Photos are stored in app-specific storage that other apps 
  cannot access
- We do not log or persist your photos on our servers

However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. 
While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee 
absolute security.
