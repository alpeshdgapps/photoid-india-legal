# Data Deletion Request — PhotoID India

**Last updated: May 25, 2026**

This page explains how users of PhotoID India can request 
deletion of their data, and what data is deleted vs retained.

## About PhotoID India

PhotoID India is a mobile application developed by Logictray Mobile Lab. 
The app helps users generate government-spec ID photos for Indian 
documents (passport, Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving License) and 
international documents (US Visa, Schengen Visa).

## What Data PhotoID India Stores

The app stores the following types of data on your device:

- **Photos**: ID photos you've captured and processed
- **Purchase records**: Records of credit packs you've purchased
- **Generated PDFs**: Print-ready PDFs you've saved

PhotoID India does NOT collect or store data on remote servers 
controlled by the developer. All your data remains on your 
device, with one exception: when you purchase a paid 
studio-quality photo, the specific photo is temporarily sent to 
Replicate (a third-party AI service) for processing and is not 
retained by Replicate after processing completes.

## How to Delete Your Data

### Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can delete all your photos directly within the app:

1. Open PhotoID India
2. Tap the **Settings** tab at the bottom of the screen
3. Tap **Clear all photos** under the Privacy section
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This permanently deletes all photos stored on your device.

### Method 2: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling PhotoID India removes all locally stored data 
including photos, purchase records, and generated PDFs.

To uninstall:
1. Go to your device's Settings → Apps
2. Find PhotoID India in the list
3. Tap Uninstall (or long-press the app icon and select Uninstall)

### Method 3: Request via Email

If you'd like to request data deletion through other means or 
have specific questions, contact us at:

**Email**: photoid.india.support@gmail.com

We will respond within 7 business days.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you use Method 1 (Clear all photos) or Method 2 (Uninstall):

**Deleted immediately:**
- All ID photos stored in the app
- All generated PDFs saved by the app
- Local cache files

**Retained:**
- **Google Play purchase records**: Records of purchases are 
  retained by Google Play Services per Google's policies. These 
  are necessary to honor purchase history if you reinstall the 
  app and use the "Restore Purchases" feature. To delete Google 
  Play purchase records, contact Google Play Support directly.

## Data Retention by Third Parties

### Replicate (AI Processing Service)

When you purchase studio-quality output, your photo is 
temporarily sent to Replicate for processing. Per Replicate's 
policy:
- Photos are processed within seconds
- Photos are NOT retained on Replicate's servers after processing 
  completes
- See Replicate's privacy policy: 
  https://replicate.com/privacy

### Google Play Services

Google Play handles all in-app purchase transactions. Purchase 
history and payment data is governed by Google Play's privacy 
policy:
- https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Account Deletion

PhotoID India does not require users to create accounts. The app 
operates without any login or registration. There is no user 
account to delete.
